ABOUT

This is the collection of works by Antoni Matheus, currently active in Brazil. Inspired by pop culture, popular art, bookstores and innovation, he is deeply fascinated by inventions, colors, movement and compositions. His work covers the cultural, advertising and commercial fields, designing and conceptualizing with excellence. Whether in the digital environment or in three-dimensional dimensions, Antoni combines several disciplines and means to create immersive and engaging experiences.
